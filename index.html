The domino effect
MeaningExampleOrigin
Chain reaction, one event triggers many others. Spilling water can cause a domino effect. Someone slips, and there is more mess everywhere. Cold War politics, "domino theory".
Wizard
MeaningExampleOrigin
Person with magical powers, often wise. The wizard makes a poison. 'Wise person', later became 'magician'.
Main character
MeaningExampleOrigin
Center of the story, main actor. Simba is the main character in "The Lion King". Greek "protagonistes": first main role.
Bibliophile
MeaningExampleOrigin
Loves/collects books, rare books. Umberto Eco is a bibliophile. He has over 50,000 books. Greek: 'biblion' + 'philos' (book lover).
Ghost kitchen
MeaningExampleOrigin
Kitchen for delivery food only, no dine-in. MrBeast Burger is a ghost kitchen: you order online, there are no restaurants. Became popular with delivery apps, COVID-19.
